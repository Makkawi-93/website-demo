Cuneo WebGIS

Project Overview

This WebGIS provides an interactive visualization of demographic and business data for the Province of Cuneo, Piedmont, Italy.

By clicking on a municipality on the map, you can explore detailed charts on population, agricultural, manufacturing and transport firms.

  • 250 municipalities of the Province of Cuneo
  • Demographic data by age group
  • Business data by sector (Agriculture, Manufacturing, Transport)

Data Source

The data used in this project comes from the following official sources:

  • ISTAT – Municipal demographic data 2022/2023
  • Business Registry – Active business locations 2025
  • OpenStreetMap – Base map
  • Regione Piemonte – Administrative boundaries

About Us

This project was developed to provide a territorial analysis tool for the Province of Cuneo.

Built with QGIS, Leaflet.js and Chart.js.

Help

  • Click on a municipality on the map to see data in the right panel
  • Use the IT/EN buttons at the top right to switch language
  • Use the layer panel (icon at top right of map) to toggle layers
  • Use the measure tool to measure distances and areas on the map
  • Click ✕ in the municipality panel to return to the overview
